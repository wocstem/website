About WOCSTEM

Our Mission

Women of Color in STEM at MSU empowers, uplifts, and connects women of color in STEM fields. We aim to create a supportive community through events, workshops, mentorship, and storytelling.

Our Vision

We envision a future where women of color in STEM thrive in inclusive spaces, are celebrated for their achievements, and feel seen, heard, and supported throughout their academic and professional journeys.

Why We Exist

STEM fields have historically lacked representation for women of color. Our organization was founded to address this gap, foster community, and champion success stories while supporting one another through shared experiences.
