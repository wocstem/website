Get Involved
WOCSTEM is powered by passionate women in STEM, and we’d love for you to be part of it. Discover how you can get involved and grow alongside this incredible community.

Become a Member


Complete the member interest form to become an official member of WOCSTEM MSU! As a member, you’ll gain access to exclusive events, including socials, networking opportunities, and professional development workshops.

Become a Sponser


Contact us to partner with WOCSTEM MSU as a sponsor! Sponsors have the opportunity to present to our diverse group of STEM leaders, promote their brand, and help create meaningful opportunities.
